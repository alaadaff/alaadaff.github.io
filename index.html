Hi there, I'm Alaa Daffalla!

My name is Alaa, and I'm a first year PhD in Computer Science student at Cornell advised by Thomas Ristenpart and Nicola Dell. I'm based in NYC at the Cornell Tech campus. My interests are broadly in usable and applied security and privacy. In a previous life I did research on optimization and hyper-heuristics. I received my Bachelors degree in Electrical Engineering from the University of Khartoum and my Masters in Computer Science from the University of Kansas.
