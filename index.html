Biography

My name is Alaa Daffalla, and I'm a third-year PhD in Computer Science student at Cornell advised by Thomas Ristenpart and Nicola Dell. I'm based in NYC at the Cornell Tech campus. My research focuses on usable and applied security and privacy.
