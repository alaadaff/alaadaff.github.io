Biography

My name is Alaa Daffalla, and I'm a third-year PhD in Computer Science student at Cornell University advised by Thomas Ristenpart and Nicola Dell. I'm based in NYC at the Cornell Tech campus. I'm broadly interested in usable and applied security and privacy. More recently my research has focused on the detection of online tech abuse and designing new interventions for abuse mitigation and prevention.
